Introduction

Guangdong OPPO Mobile Telecommunications, commonly referred to as OPPO, is a Chinese consumer electronics and mobile communications company headquartered in Dongguan, Guangdong, China, best known for its smartphones, Blu-ray players and other electronic devices. OPPO is the leading smartphone company, and one of the best smartphone brands in China in 2019, ranked 5th globally. It is a subsidiary of BBK Electronics along with Vivo and Realme OnePlus

The ABU brand was registered in China in 2001, and launched in 2004. Since then, it has expanded to all over the world. In June 2016, OPPO became the largest smartphone manufacturer in China, selling its phones in more than 200,000 retail outlets.
