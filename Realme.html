Introduction

Realme is a Chinese smartphone manufacturer headquartered in Shenzhen. The brand was officially founded on May 4, 2018 (China National Youth Day) by Sky Li (born in Pinghong), who was formerly Vice President of Oppo. In addition to smartphones, realme also produces a wide range of other products such as earphones, fitness bands and bags.

Realme debuted in China in 2010 under the name "OPPO Real", as it was a sub-brand of OPPO until it broke up in 2018, after which it became its own brand under the Chinese multinational company BBK Electronics. On July 30, 2018, OPPO's former Vice President and Head of OPPO Overseas Business Department Sky Lee announced his official resignation from OPPO and his intention to establish realme as an independent brand on Chinese microblogging site Sina Weibo. He said that in the future, the realme brand will focus on providing mobile phones that combine powerful performance and elegant design, thus providing young people with a happy life characterized by “technology” and “beauty” at affordable prices.
